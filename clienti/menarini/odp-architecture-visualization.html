Rejection Behavior:
‚Ä¢ Any approver (SIGNER_1, SIGNER_2, PAYER) can reject an ODP
‚Ä¢ Rejection immediately stops the approval workflow
‚Ä¢ Rejected ODPs cannot be edited (stato.isOdpCanBeEdited() = false for REJECTED)
‚Ä¢ Owner must create a new ODP or system may allow returning to DRAFT (implementation-dependent)
